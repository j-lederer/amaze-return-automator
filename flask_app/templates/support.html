Frequently Asked Questions

How do I search for a return?

Enter the tracking ID in the search field on the dashboard and click Search. The system will retrieve all relevant return information.

What does the processing queue do?

The queue holds returns that are ready for processing. You can add returns to the queue and then batch process them.

How do I refresh data?

Click the 'Refresh Data' button to sync with the latest information from your external systems.

Can I download reports?

Yes, use the 'Download Report' button to generate and download processing reports.
