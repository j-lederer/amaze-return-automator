Frequently Asked Questions

How do I search for returns?

Simply scan the barcode or enter the tracking ID in the search field on the main dashboard.

What happens when I add items to the processing queue?

Items in the queue will be automatically processed at 12:00 AM EST daily for restocking.

How often does the data refresh?

Data is refreshed automatically every hour. You can also manually refresh using the "Refresh Data" button.

Can I download reports?

Yes, you can download both full PDF reports and slim reports using the download buttons on the dashboard.

How do I clear the queue?

Use the "Clear Queue" button to remove all items, or use the trash icon next to individual items to remove them one by one.
